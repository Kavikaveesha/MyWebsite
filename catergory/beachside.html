Topic1

Ancient city Anuradhapura

The Kingdom of Anuradhapura holds great fame and splendor. Anuradhapura, Sri Lanka's third and longest-serving capital, is one of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities and holds immense significance for Buddhists worldwide. It served as the capital of Sri Lanka from the 4th century BC until the turn of the 11th century and stood as a stable and enduring center of political power and urban life in South Asia. It is worth noting that the city itself predates the establishment of the Kingdom of Anuradhapura and may have originated as far back as the 10th century BC, as suggested by archaeological evidence. The city was eventually abandoned due to repeated invasions from South India and remained forgotten until its rediscovery in the early 19th century

Topic2

Ancient city Polonnaruwa

Polonnaruwa, the second most ancient kingdom in Sri Lanka, became the political center of the country in the 9th century BC. The irrigation systems developed during the Polonnaruwa kingdom surpassed those of the Anuradhapura Age and continue to provide water for irrigation to farmers in the region. However, the kingdom's glory was short-lived, as it faced a South Indian invasion, which shifted the political center toward the central part of the country by the 11th century. Presently, the ancient city of Polonnaruwa stands as one of the best-planned archaeological sites in Sri Lanka, showcasing the discipline and grandeur of the kingdom's early rulers

Topic3

Ancient city Kandy

Kandy is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the last capital of the ancient kings of Sri Lanka. It is known for the Temple of the Tooth, which houses a sacred tooth relic of the Buddha. Kandy also hosts the Esala Perahera, a grand cultural procession featuring traditional dancers, drummers, and decorated elephants.







Heritage places

of srilanka

1.Sigiriya

Sigiriya is a famous heritage site in Sri Lanka. Also known as the Lion Rock, it is an ancient rock fortress with stunning frescoes and panoramic views. This historic site showcases the country's rich history and architectural grandeur, attracting visitors from around the globe.

2. Ruwanwelisaya

Ruwanwelisaya is a revered Buddhist stupa located in Sri Lanka. It is a significant religious and cultural landmark known for its magnificent architectural design and spiritual importance. The stupa is believed to enshrine sacred relics of Lord Buddha, making it a popular pilgrimage site for Buddhists.
